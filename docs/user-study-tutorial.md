# User Study Tutorial

Please follow these steps in order:

1. Open the application using the provided link.
2. Enter the app by either selecting a demo profile or typing a display name and continuing.
3. Explore the app briefly and get a sense of what it seems to let you do.
4. Find a sports session that looks interesting or suitable for you.
5. Open that session and review its details.
6. Join the session if it seems like a good fit.
7. Send a short message in the session chat, for example introducing yourself or asking a simple question.
8. Return to the main browsing area and create a new sports session.
9. Fill in the session details as if you were organizing a real activity.
10. Review the session information before completing the flow.
11. Find your profile and review or update your information or preferences.
12. Explore the application freely for around 3 to 5 minutes.

## Important

- Try to use the system naturally.
- Do not overthink your actions.
- Focus on your first impressions.
- If you are unsure what to do next, continue in the way that feels most natural to you.
- There are no right or wrong answers.
